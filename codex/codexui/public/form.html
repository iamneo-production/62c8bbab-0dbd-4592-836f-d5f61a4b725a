Predict AQI

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a numerical rating system used to communicate how polluted the air is in a particular area. The AQI typically ranges from 0 to 500, with higher numbers indicating worse air quality.

  • AQI values of 0 to 50 are considered Good
  • AQI values of 51 to 100 are considered Moderate
  • AQI values of 101 to 200 are considered Poor
  • AQI values of 201 to 300 are considered Unhealthy
  • AQI values of 301 to 400 are considered Severe
  • AQI values of 401 to 500 are considered Hazardous

  • AQI values above 100 are considered unhealthy for sensitive groups
  • AQI values above 150 are considered unhealthy for everyone
