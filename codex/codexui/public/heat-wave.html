Predict Heat Wave

Heat wave is a condition of air temperature which becomes fatal to human body when exposed. Quantitatively, it is defined based on the temperature thresholds over a region in terms of actual temperature or its departure from normal.

  • Green Alert -> Normal Day
  • Yellow Alert (Be updated) -> Caution
  • Orange Alert (Be prepared) -> Extreme Caution
  • Orangish Brown Alert (Take Action) -> Severe
  • Red Alert (Take Action) -> Danger

Predict The Heat Waves here by entering the name to City, Year, Month, and Rainfall, Minimum Temperature, Max Temperature, Minimum Humidity, Maximum Humidity, Maximum Wind Speed, Minimum Wind Speed
